Freecell

Since the beginning of time, people have wasted time on the fun, tireless game of Freecell. Since Windows 95, freecell.exe has been in the C:\Windows\system32 folder, until Windows Vista. Vista ruined everything. Lives, businesses, relationships, UAC and other 'features' wrecked havoc on the world and Microsoft's stock, it even ruined freecell.exe. Today, balance has been restored, as the original freecell.exe has been attained and is now readily available, if you are willing to work.

First, download the .zip and unzip it.

Next, open cmd inside the folder.

Run: expand freecell.ex_ freecell.exe and expand cards.dl_ cards.dll

Move those two files into a directory you'd like to keep Freecell. Done!
